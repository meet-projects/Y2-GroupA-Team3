We Are Ukko

Ukko has developed a platform based on computational biology, patient samples, immunology, and protein engineering to tackle the global food allergy epidemic. Our innovative approach aims to neutralize allergenic elements while maintaining immunogenicity, which allows us to create a safe and effective treatment without disrupting the quality of life for those with food allergies. Additionally, our research opens up the possibility of more convenient routes of administration, akin to allergy shots, and holds promise for a future multi-allergen approach.
